In this podcast with Paul Laursen, we discuss what AI can realistically do in training and what it cannot. We explore the importance of considering ethical and practical constraints when applying AI to training, and how Athletica integrates scientific knowledge while accounting for subjective factors and personal experience.

For more details, see Paul's [blog post](https://athletica.ai/athletica-coach-version-a-new-tool-to-leverage-the-hiit-science-principles/).
